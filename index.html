    How to use

    Tap any pin or list item for the full rundown — hours, status and details. Use the filters to show or hide categories and switch days to view the schedule. Items marked soon are confirmed for 2026 but lineup or schedule hasn't been posted yet. Whoop whoop!

    Sponsorship Opportunities

    The app is an official channel in every attendee’s pocket — live on the grounds, all weekend long. Here’s how partners and vendors plug in.

    Flagship 🏆
    Official App Sponsor

    Title the whole experience — “The Official GOTJ App, presented by ___.” One partner, top billing on the splash screen and in every session, in front of the entire on-site audience.

    📣
    Sponsored Push Alert

    Land on every fan’s phone in real time — “Late-night eats open now by the main stage.” A notification, not an ad they have to go looking for.

    ⭐
    Featured Vendor Listing

    Vendors upgrade to a full profile: logo, description, category tag, and priority placement in the in-app directory.

    📍
    Sponsored Map Pin

    A branded, stand-out pin on the interactive grounds map. Food, merch, tattoo, drinks, services — get found first.

    🛒
    Merch & Ticket Links

    One-tap links to official tickets, upgrades, merch, camping & RV packages, and vendor applications — attention turned into clicks.

    Flexible packages — from a single featured vendor to the presenting partner. Built to scale with the Gathering.
